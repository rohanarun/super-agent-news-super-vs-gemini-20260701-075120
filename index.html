Why computer use matters now

Gemini is pushing hard

Google has made computer use a first‑class feature in Gemini 3.5 Flash and is rolling Gemini Spark into its macOS app, underscoring how valuable real desktop control has become.

Cost compounds with repetition

Most agents re‑execute the same steps every run. Super’s computer-use cache means repeated work can improve over time instead of staying linearly expensive.

Security & realism

Once agents can operate computers, attackers adapt quickly — making intentional design, scope control, and observability critical.

Demand is real

Interest in personal AI agents is growing globally, especially across Asia‑Pacific, signalling durable demand beyond novelty chatbots.
